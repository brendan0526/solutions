Our Story

Why we started MDL.

MDL Insurance Solutions was founded on a straightforward idea: most people are either overpaying for coverage they don't need, underinsured for risks they don't realize they have, or both. A captive agent at a single carrier can't fix that problem — they can only sell what their company offers.

As an independent agency, we have access to 100+ carrier markets across personal and commercial lines. That means we can actually shop your risk and bring you options — not just the product our employer tells us to push.

We serve Phoenix, Scottsdale, Mesa, Goodyear, and the broader Arizona market. We're licensed, local, and pick up the phone.

Our Commitment

We shop, you save

We run your risk across multiple carriers every time — at new business and at renewal. Rates change. We make sure you're not stuck paying last year's price.

Plain language, always

We explain what your policy actually covers — and what it doesn't — in terms you can understand. No jargon, no fine print surprises.

We're here when it matters

Claims happen. When they do, you want a real person who knows your policy and can help you navigate the process. That's us.
